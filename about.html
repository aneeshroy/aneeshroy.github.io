ABOUT ME

I'm Aneesh, but all my friends call me Chiko. Growing up in Coppell, Texas, a suburb of Dallas, I've embraced the Southern hospitality I see all around me.

With the multitude of things I am interested in, its hard to pinpoint what makes me who I am. I think the best description comes from my parents, who've molded me from the bottom up. My earliest memories have me responding to two different names; Aneesh for the academic demands, and Chiko for everything else. I didn’t come to this unusual realization that I lived with two personas until I was older, and began to see how my two names morphed into two very different sides of me. I always thought one side would win over the years, but I understand now that long ago they stopped fighting and started coexisting, an outcome I cherish every day.

I've engrossed myself in learning anything and everything with the hopes of eventually becoming a Jeopardy champion. In my free time, I play the drums and love to produce music. In high school, my band Flood the Walls released an album I linked below. I watch and play basketball, and love to rock climb and swim as well. I will go to every concert I am remotely interested in, and will basically try anything once.
